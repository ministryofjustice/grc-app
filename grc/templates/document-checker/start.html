Before you start

Use this tool to check which documents you need for your Gender Recognition Certificate application.

Answering the questions means we can give you a list of documents based on your personal circumstances. You can have the list of documents sent to you by email at the end.

This is not part of the application process and is designed only to help you prepare the documents you need before you start your application.

We do not store any of the information you enter in this tool.

If you already have your documents ready you can start your application.
